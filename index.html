Helping people in need

We are a non-profit organization helping people who are in need of blood or plasma by connecting them to people who are willing and are eligible to donate it!

More details Sign up

Connecting donors and recipients is our goal. We save your Time and Energy!

Finding donors is really tough especially the one that matches your specific requirements. We at IAmADonor try our best to solve this problem and connect donors who are eligible & willing to donate to people in need! As a donor you get to select the recipient to whom you'd like to donate.
